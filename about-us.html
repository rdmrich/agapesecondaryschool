About Us

HISTORY

William Nsubuga moved to the United States after surviving the Rwanda Genocide in 1996. He never forgot his desire to help out people with HIV/AIDS and affected distitute children. William lived in (place) Alabama and started a successful Construction and Home Improvement Business for ten (10) years.

After several visits to Africa, William felt touched with the number of Aids orphaned kids and affected children in Uganda. In 2008, William founded Agape Total Childcare Centre in Uganda after abanding is business in the United States of America. Doors opened for children aged six (6) to Twelve (12) years old who lost one or both parants to HIV/AIDS and had no place to live.

William, envisioned Agape being a christian home for those children, a place where they are safe, feel loved, attend school and recieve medical Education when needed. He eventually wanted to open a school for both residents of Agape and local student. With the help of Precios partner and friends, Agape moved from a limited space in Lugazi that was not conducive for studying or sleeping. William bought land in Mukono and 2011 the first Missia Teams from USA arrived to begin working on facilities for the new childcare center, which would include an administration building, domiteries and a secondary school.
